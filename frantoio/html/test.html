Ever Evolving

Our menus are an ever evolving representation of the expertise and dedication of Chef Duilio Valenti, sous chef John “Pupone” Eitel and their kitchen staff. We take great pride in sourcing only the finest ingredients to use in our menu. Organic, locally produced fruit, vegetables, dairy, seafood and meat. This means you get the freshest ingredients available that not only taste amazing, they also help reduce the environmental impact of transporting food thousands of miles.

All dishes are prepared with our own award winning extra virgin olive oil, pressed at the restaurant, and all pastas, pizzas, breads and gelatos are made in house daily.

All natural, all the time. Buon appetito.
